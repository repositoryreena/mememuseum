← Back to Home

Hi! I spend way too much of my free time looking at memes on Pinterest— the niche ones, the vintage ones, the deeply cursed and oddly beautiful ones.

I used to send memes individually to all my friends, but it got out of control. Like, who has the energy to forward 1,000 memes to 12 different people?

So I decided to turn my studio apartment into a full-blown **meme museum**—with real prints on every wall—and this site is the digital version. A place where anyone can scroll through the chaos, the beauty, and the drama.

Hope you enjoy. Send this to someone who deserves art today.
